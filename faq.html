FAQ

Answers for customers, students, and payment processor review

These answers clearly explain that the business sells digital educational content related to video game development and that the website provides transparent business information.

What does Forgeframe Academy sell?+

Forgeframe Academy sells digital educational products focused on video game development, including course videos, practical tutorials, project breakdowns, devlogs, and behind-the-scenes creator lessons.

How is content delivered?+

Content is delivered digitally through online access to educational materials and video-based learning resources.

Do customers receive physical goods?+

No. The platform sells digital educational content only and does not ship physical products.

What experience level is this for?+

It is designed for beginners, aspiring game developers, indie creators, and anyone who wants to learn through real project-based content.

Why is the creator journey part of the product?+

The creator journey provides context, transparency, and real-world lessons. It turns progress, iteration, and decision making into a practical educational experience.

Where can customers review policies or contact support?+

Customers can review Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, Refund Policy, and contact information on the Contact page.

Contact and business policies

Transparent business information for students and payment review

Business clarity statement

Forgeframe Academy sells digital educational content related to video game development, including course videos, tutorials, project breakdowns, devlogs, and creator-led learning materials. Customers purchase access to online educational products and not physical goods.

Terms of Service

By purchasing or accessing content from Forgeframe Academy, customers agree that all offerings are digital educational products related to video game development learning. Access is granted for educational use only and may not be redistributed, resold, copied, or republished without permission. The business may update the platform, lessons, or access structure over time.

Privacy Policy

The business may collect basic customer information necessary for account access, communication, purchases, analytics, and support. Customer data should be handled responsibly and not sold in ways inconsistent with normal business operations. Visitors should be informed about email signups, order processing, and contact forms.

Refund Policy

Because products are digital educational materials delivered through online access, refund eligibility should be clearly explained. A common approach is to state that refunds may be reviewed on a case-by-case basis if access issues occur, while emphasizing that digital content can be immediately accessible after purchase. Final wording should match the business's exact practice.

Support Policy

Support requests should be submitted through the listed contact email. Responses can cover billing questions, access issues, and product clarification. Clear communication and response expectations help build trust for both customers and payment processors.
